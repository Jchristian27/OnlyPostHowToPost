How to Post Pictures & Videos Using OnlyPost

  1. Open a new terminal window.
  2. Type post and hit enter.
  3. Type out the caption for your photo or videoGIF and hit enter.
  4. Copy the URL of the imgur photo or RedGifs video that you want to post, paste it into the terminal, and hit enter.
  5. A list of genres will be presented to you. Determine what genre the content you're about to post falls under, type it into the terminal, and hit enter.
  6. Leave the terminal open until the script is done executing. When the script is done executing a message that says All done! You can now close the terminal window :) will be printed out onto the terminal.
  7. That's it! Close the terminal window and repeat the next time you want to post a photo or videoGIF.
